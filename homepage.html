App

Want to take a break? Take a look at different destinations at Baguio City using Jeepy-es and find what place interests you. Scan the QR Code and download now your virtual Baguio City tour guide, Jeepy-es at your service!


Jeepy-es will facilitate tourists in getting around Baguio by providing a user-friendly website and mobile app. This platform will offer valuable information, including the location of jeepney terminals at various tourist destinations, jeep fare details, estimated travel times, landmarks indicating where to disembark from the jeepney, and comprehensive information about the available jeepneys. This information will encompass details such as passenger capacity, license plate numbers, jeep color, names of specific jeep routes, and overall descriptions of the different types of jeepneys available.
